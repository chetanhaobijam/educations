About Us
Education Department of Manipur originated back in 1903 when Rev. Pettigrew an educationist and a philanthropist became the first honorary inspected of schools under the Education Department of Assam. On 20th January 1950 a separate Department of Education was created for the first time in Manipur with Shri T. Kipgen as Assistant Secretary to the Government of Manipur.
